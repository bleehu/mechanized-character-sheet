Medium Water

Deep Water

 -3 -3 3 Hexes

Reduces incoming nuclear damage by 3d4. The intense pressure of water at this depth deals 1d4 damage that ignores shields at the end of the player's turn.

Rough Terrain

 None None 4 Hexes

Buildings

 +2 Cannot be shot through Cannot be moved through

Buildings cannot be moved through or shot through. A mech with Jump Jets or the Quantumn Teleporter can get on top of buildings. While on top of a building, a mech has a +1 to accuracy rolls, and all mechs targeting it get a +1.

Lava

 -1 -1 1 Hex

For each lava hex entered, a mech takes 2d4+1 damage of the same type as flamethrowers.

Nuclear Power Plant

 0 0 0

The Nuclear Power Plant has 10 hp. If you destroy the power plant, it explodes as if it has a generator level = 2+ (2* round#).

Nuclear Submarine

 0 0 0

The Nuclear Sub has 10 hp. If you destroy the sub, it explodes as if it has a generator level = 3 + (2* round#).
